Wheels on the road

So, who am I? Well, for now, just call me Jayren, I'm just like many of you out there – completely and utterly smitten with motorcycles. It started with a childhood fascination, the roar of engines echoing in my dreams.  

Ever feel that pull? That irresistible urge to twist the throttle, feel the wind in your face, and let the world blur into a beautiful, adrenaline-fueled landscape? Yeah, me too. That feeling is what sparked this whole crazy idea – a blog by a newbie, for the love of all things motorcycle.

Riding Through Taguig

Early mornings in Taguig hit different—empty streets, soft sunrise light, and the low purr of the Winner X ready to roll. This weekend, I decided to beat the traffic and just ride. No destination, just me, the road, and the Winner X.

From Bonifacio Global City to the riverside roads near C5, this underbone beast handled the mix of smooth tarmac and sudden potholes like a champ. Lightweight and quick on the throttle, it made weaving through the city feel like play.
